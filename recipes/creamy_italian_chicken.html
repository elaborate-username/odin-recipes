Creamy Italian Chicken Pasta

This recipes gives intstructions to makes a delicious creamy pasta sauce with chicken for two people. It takes about 30 minutes to complete.

Ingredients for 2 people

  • Pasta of you choice (Penne recommended)
  • Sun dried tomatoes
  • 3 Garlic cloves
  • Chicken bits
  • Creme Fraiche
  • Mustard
  • Parmezan cheese
  • Herbs and spices

Instructions

  1. Cook the stuff.
  2. Chop the stuff.
  3. Put the stuff together.
  4. Eat the stuff.
