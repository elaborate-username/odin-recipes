Chicken Pesto Salad

This recipes gives intstructions to makes a delicious salad with pasta, pesto and chicken for two people. It takes about 30 minutes to complete.

Ingredients for 2 people

  • Pasta of you choice (Penne recommended)
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Veldsla
  • Chicken bits
  • Creme Fraiche
  • Pesto
  • Paprika
  • Mozarella
  • Onion

Instructions

  1. Cook the stuff.
  2. Chop the stuff.
  3. Put the stuff together.
  4. Eat the stuff.
