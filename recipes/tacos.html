Tacos

This recipes gives intstructions to makes tacos for two people. It takes about 30 minutes to complete.

Ingredients for 2 people

  • Taco shells (hard or soft)
  • Avocado
  • Spring onions
  • Veggie minced meat
  • Creme Fraiche
  • Paprika
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Fucked up cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook the stuff.
  2. Chop the stuff.
  3. Put the stuff together.
  4. Eat the stuff.
